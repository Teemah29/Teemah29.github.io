About Us!!!

House of Bling Home of Fashion takes delight in sophisticated appearance and quality. We keep abreast of latest fashion trends and we also introduce new trends into the fashion world. House of Bling came into existence in 2012 and today we are a top ranking fashion house across the Globe.

We specialise in women world of fashion ranging from Gowns to Trousers, Jumpsuits, Tops, Jwelries, Bags and Shoes. we provide ready made designs and we also tailor our designs to your precise taste, you just need to give us a decription of what you want.

We also provide support to upcoming fashion designers who need assistance on producing certain designs. All you just need to do is upload a picture of the design you are trying to achieve and we will send you a video on how to produce the design with your fabric.

To request for support and also make orders, download our app on Google PlayStore "HouseofBling_Mobile"
